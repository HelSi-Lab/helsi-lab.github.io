# Join Us

HelSi Lab welcomes students interested in bio/healthcare systems, simulation-based
optimization, digital twins, and data-driven modeling.
학부 연구생 모집 — 시뮬레이션 · 최적화 · 디지털 트윈에 관심 있는 학생을 찾습니다.

## Who we're looking for

- Undergraduates with curiosity and persistence who want to learn how research works
- Interest in bio/healthcare systems, simulation-based optimization, or digital twins
- Preferred: comfort with programming (Python or similar)
- 2nd–4th year students — about 2–3 positions

## What you'll do

- Explore research ideas and work toward writing papers
- Join a vacation journal club (this year's theme: agent-based simulation)
- Opportunities to publish, present at conferences, and join international collaborations
- Scholarship support when participating in funded projects

## How to apply

Email Prof. Serin Lee with a short note about your interests and background.
An informal chat follows, then final selection.
